Breakfast Menu

Entrees

  • Frittata: An egg-based casserole that can be customized with various fillings like sausage, potatoes, vegetables, and cheese.
  • Omelets and scrambles: A classic omelet can be filled with cheese, ham, or mushrooms. Scrambles can be a quick one-pan meal with eggs, sausage, potatoes, and vegetables.
  • Breakfast hash: A hearty skillet meal typically made with diced potatoes, onions, peppers, and breakfast sausage or corned beef.
  • Biscuits and gravy: A Southern classic featuring warm, fluffy biscuits smothered in a creamy white gravy with crumbled sausage.

Beverages

  • Coke Products: Coke, Sprite, Hi-C, Dr. Pepper, Verners, Diet Coke, Coke Zero, Fanta Orange
  • Other: Milk, Chocolate Milk, Lemonade, Iced Tea, Orange Juice
  • Caffeine: Coffee, Frappe (caramel or mocha)

Dinner Menu

Entrees

  • Quesadillas: Layer cheese and your choice of filling (like chicken, beans, or vegetables) between tortillas and cook in a skillet until golden and crispy.
  • Salmon with roasted vegetables: Bake salmon fillets on a sheet pan with your choice of vegetables, such as asparagus, broccoli, or sweet potatoes.
  • Quinoa Salad Bowls: Combine cooked quinoa with fresh vegetables, a lean protein like steak or chicken, and a zesty vinaigrette for a refreshing and healthy bowl.
