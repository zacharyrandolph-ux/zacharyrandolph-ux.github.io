Quarterbacks

The Team



Tom Brady

Tom Brady is a former quarterback who used to play for the New England Patriots and Tampa Bay Buccaneers. Brady has many records, including most career passing yards, completions, touchdown passes, and games started. He played for 23 seasons and was the oldest player to win a Super Bowl MVP at the age of 43. After retiring in 2023, he became a lead color commentator for the NFL on Fox and a partial owner of the Las Vegas Raiders. Over the years, he has led his team to the superbowl 9 times, and won 6. He is estimated to have a net worth of 300 million. People often think of him as the best quarterback of all time.

Drew Breeze

Drew Breeze was born on January 15, 1979, in Dallas, Texas. His father played basketball at Texas A&M, and his mom was an all-state athlete in 3 sports. This made him want to play a professional sport, which is why he started football. He played from 2001-2020, and played for 20 seasons in the NFL. During that time he mostly played for the New Orlean Saints. He has won a superbowl and has been awared a few MVP's. After retiring, Brees became a football analyst and is currently a color commentator for NFL games on FOX. He also spends time with his kids and his wife named Brittney Brees.


Dak Prescott

Dak was born on July 29, 1993, in Sulphur, Louisiana. He played in college for the Mississippi State Bulldogs. He was later drafted in the 4th round by the Dallas Cowboys. He has been selected to the Pro Bowl 3 times. He has set many records and has many honors. He is known for having a strong arm and for being a great leader. He was intended to be a backup, but Tony Romo got injured, and Dak got to fill in his position. He is engaged to Sarah Jane Ramos, and have 2 children.


Patrick Mahomes

Patrick Maholmes played for the Kansas City Chiefs. He is a good player who has brought his team to 3 Super Bowl wins. He holds many records and also has many MVP awards. He started off at Texas Tech, where he was drafted as a first-round pick in 2017. Off the field, he is a husband and father. He runs a foundation that helps the underprivileged youth.
